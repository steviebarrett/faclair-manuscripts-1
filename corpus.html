Manuscript Details: Edinburgh NLS Adv. 72.1.33

A 16th-century medical and astological manuscript, mostly written by members of the Beaton medical family.

This transcription file currently includes: (1) 11 charms from the first half of the manuscript; (2) Excerpts from the Latin translation of Averroes's Colliget, with Gaelic translation; (3) A letter from Lachlan Mór MacLean of Duart to his physician.
